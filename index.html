P.U.L.S. — Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-07-02

P.U.L.S. is an internal tool used within Atende Industries to work with data from Jira.

  • It runs fully locally on your own device.
  • It sends no data anywhere except your own direct connection to Atlassian (Jira).
  • Any data it stores stays on your device and can be cleared at any time using the app's log out button.
  • It does not share your data with any third party.
  • It does not collect analytics or usage statistics.
  • It does not track you or serve advertising.

For any questions about this app, contact the DSZE team on Slack or by email.
